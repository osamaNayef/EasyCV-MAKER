# EasyCV Maker

## User Guide

### Introduction

EasyCV Maker is an ATS-friendly resume builder that helps users create professional resumes quickly and efficiently.

---

## Step 1: Personal Information

Fill in:

* Full Name
* Job Title
* Email Address
* Phone Number
* Location

Optional:

* Date of Birth
* Marital Status
* Gender

---

## Step 2: Education

Enter:

* Degree & Major
* University Name
* GPA / Average
* Graduation Year

---

## Step 3: Professional Summary

Write a short summary describing:

* Your experience
* Key achievements
* Professional strengths

Recommended length:
3–6 lines.

---

## Step 4: Work Experience

Click "Add Experience".

For each position enter:

* Company Name
* Job Title
* Start Date
* End Date
* Responsibilities and Achievements

You may add unlimited work experience entries.

---

## Step 5: Skills

List your:

* Technical Skills
* Soft Skills
* Professional Competencies

Example:

• PHP
• SQL
• Linux
• Teamwork
• Problem Solving

---

## Step 6: Hobbies & Interests

Add optional interests that support your professional profile.

Example:

• Reading
• Volunteering
• Technology Research

---

## Step 7: Digital Links

Add:

* LinkedIn Profile
* GitHub Profile

These links help recruiters review your professional portfolio.

---

## Step 8: Choose Your Design

Select:

* One of 4 professional layouts
* One of 10 professional color themes

The system automatically generates one of 40 ATS-compliant resume styles.

---

## Step 9: Preview Your Resume

Review the live preview section.

Ensure:

* No spelling mistakes
* Accurate contact information
* Professional formatting

---

## Step 10: Export to PDF

Click:

"Save as PDF"

The system generates a clean ATS-friendly PDF ready for job applications.

---

## Best Practices

✔ Use professional language.
✔ Keep information accurate.
✔ Focus on achievements.
✔ Use relevant keywords for your target job.
✔ Keep the resume concise and easy to read.

Thank you for using EasyCV Maker.
